Pick a role and get your task.
Let's solve this crisis!

U.S. President

U.S.President: typical politician

TASK:

try to have a conversation with your enemy so that you can reach an agreement and solve the crisis together.

REMEMBER:

The Soviet Union'leader has a weird sense of humour!

But he already has the Doomsday Machine ready, so don't anger him either, otherwise he will trigger the Doomsday even before the U.S. planes arrive.

You will anger him, if:

you make him wait in the conversation,

you pick the wrong thing to say.

decode guy

Officer at the base: you are the only one that could figure out the recall code of the attack

TASK:

figure out a 3-letter-code to stop the attack on Soviet Union and thereby prevent the Doomsday.

REMEMBER:

use only letters for the code

no duplicate letters

the order of the letters matters, you'll get hint along the way

U.S. Pilot

U.S. Pilot: obedient soldier, follow all the orders

TASK:

Hold on to the bomb while it moves.

Press the arrow key in the same direction as the bomb's movement

.

REMEMBER:

There is a demo before the game starts.

You have 3 lives in this game.

Russia Airforce

Soviet Union pilot: you are trying to stop the U.S. planes.

TASK:

shoot down the planes as fast as you can

REMEMBER:

you can't fire continuously

you can use a/s/d/w as arrow keys as well to pan over and look for the planes.

You start with 4 planes to shoot.
